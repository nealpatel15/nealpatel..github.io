Tips and Experiences on Overcoming Body Image Struggles on Social Media

Our podcast is about six college students from different backgrounds talking about social media’s influence on self-image. We are all in the same age group as our audience and are all avid users of social media, making us familiar with its beauty standards. Having experienced the difficulties of positive body image due to social media, we want to share our experiences and how we’ve overcome these struggles or are in the process of doing so.
